SecureWallet is a service that allows people to transfer money to each other and to small business with low fees and no chargebacks. The goal is a fast and easy signup process where the only personal information you submit is your email address instead of your full name, address, social security number, and other personal information as is commonly required by other internet payment providers.

When you are ready to transfer or receive money, you can link a bank account number to your account. You can delete any of your information or your whole account at any time. We will collect the minimum information required and encrypt as much of it as possible in a way that only you can access it. We achieve lower fees per transaction by only using bank accounts to transfer funds and not accepting credit cards.
